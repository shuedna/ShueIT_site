Master Recipe

Only 5 Ingredients!!

  1. 3 cups Flour (Regular or Bread)
  2. 1 1/2 cup Water
  3. 1 tablespoon Salt
  4. 1 tablespoon Butter
  5. 2 1/4 teaspoons Yeast (1 packet)

Step 1

Mix Flour and Salt in large bowl

Wisk Warm Water, Melted Butter, Yeast and optional pinch of sugar

Let water mixture sit for a few minutes


Step 2

Mix Wet and Dry mixtures by hand with wooden spoon.

mix gently untill dough collects into a ball.

Dust with flour and by hand press down to flatten into bowl

Scrape out dough flip over dust with flour again and flatten into bowl

Step 3

Cover bowl loosly with plastic wrap

Place dough some where warm to raise where it will not be disurbed.

Allow to raise one to two hours. the dough should double in size

Step 4

Take a pizza peel or cookie sheet and dust with pleanty of corn meal.

(if using a pan/cookie sheet you can litely oil if you do not have corm meal)

Scrape dough out of bowl, fold once or twice and shape into a loaf.

The dough will be very sticky, dust you hands and the dough with flour to avoid sticking

Step 5

Pre-Heat your oven (or BBQ) 425 to 405

Gently, Using a sharp knife cut three of four lines in the top of the dough about 1/4" deep

Avoid pressing down on the dough when doing this as will flatten your loaf

Brush With egg wash (just a beaten egg) if you dont have a brush paper towel will work, this is option but helps your crust

BAKE!!

Bake for 25 to 30 minutes

Until golden brown
