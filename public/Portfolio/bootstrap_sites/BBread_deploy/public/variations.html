Variations

This recipe can be used to make many different things

Just a few Ideas

  • Pizza
  • Sausage/Pepperoni Bread
    • Roll out and fill with cooked sausage muchrooms and mozzarella cheese, roll up and cut.
  • Cinnimon Buns
    • Add a teaspoon Molasses and Teaspoon of Pumpkin Pie Spice to the Dough
    • Roll out and fill with any Cinnimon bun mix, roll up and cut.
  • Rolls
    • After raising divide into six portions, use the ring lids of wide mouth ball canning Jars to hold the shape.
  • Onion Bread
    • Omit the salt and add a packet of Onion Soup mix (already quite salty)

I will Add Some Pictures as I remake these items.
